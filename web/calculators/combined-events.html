About Combined Events

Combined events are athletics competitions that consist of multiple track and field disciplines. Athletes earn points based on their performance in each event, with the winner being determined by the highest total score.

Event Types

  • Decathlon (Men): 10 events over two days - 100m, Long Jump, Shot Put, High Jump, 400m, 110m Hurdles, Discus, Pole Vault, Javelin, 1500m
  • Heptathlon (Women): 7 events over two days - 100m Hurdles, High Jump, Shot Put, 200m, Long Jump, Javelin, 800m
  • Pentathlon (Indoor): 5 events - Men: 60m Hurdles, Long Jump, Shot Put, High Jump, 1000m; Women: 60m Hurdles, High Jump, Shot Put, Long Jump, 800m

Scoring System

Each discipline uses World Athletics' official scoring tables to convert performances into points. The tables are designed to balance the scoring across different event types (running, jumping, throwing).
